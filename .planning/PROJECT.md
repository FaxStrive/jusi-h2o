# Jusi H2O

## What This Is

A premium water filtration company website for Jusi H2O, serving Tampa Bay and surrounding counties (Hillsborough, Pinellas, Manatee, Sarasota, Polk). The site positions them as a clean water lifestyle company - not a plumbing company - emphasizing free water testing, honest education, customized filtration solutions, and their JusiCare+ maintenance program. Target audience is homeowners concerned about water quality.

## Core Value

Homeowners can quickly understand their water problems, see how Jusi H2O solves them, and schedule a free in-home water test with zero friction.
